## Маңызды ескертулер / Important notes

1. **Data sources and freshness.** All contact information is from publicly available professional directories: the official Kazakhstan bar registry at advokat.kz, the e-government open data portal (data.egov.kz via findh.org, updated July 2025), 2GIS map service, spravker.ru, individual law firm websites, and the US Embassy attorney list (July 2024). Phone numbers and addresses may change; verification before outreach is recommended.

2. **Specialization labeling.** Many Kazakh lawyers, especially in smaller cities, practice across multiple areas of law. Where the registry listed only "адвокатская деятельность" without specific specialization, the entry is marked as "general practice" or inferred from the firm's stated services.

3. **Email availability.** Individual lawyer emails are rarely published in Kazakhstan's bar registries. Emails are most consistently available for law firms and bar association chairpersons. For individual lawyers without listed emails, contact is typically via phone/WhatsApp.

4. **Key online registries for additional lookups:**
   - **findh.org** — most comprehensive, searchable by all 20 regions, based on egov.kz data
   - **advokat.kz/spisok-advokatov-kazahstana/** — organized by region with full phone/address
   - **yur.kz** and **yuristy.kz** — lawyer marketplace with ratings and reviews
   - **2gis.kz** — map-based search for lawyers in any Kazakhstan city
   - **adilet.kz** — search by specialization and city

5. **International firms for corporate/tax/energy matters.** GRATA International operates offices in **18 Kazakhstan cities** (including Aktau, Atyrau, Aktobe, Karaganda, Kostanay, Pavlodar, Petropavlovsk, Shymkent, Taraz, Uralsk, and Ust-Kamenogorsk). Their regional contact numbers are accessible via https://gratanet.com/ru/regions/kazakhstan. Baker McKenzie, Dentons, Kinstellar, White & Case, and Morgan Lewis cover primarily Almaty and Astana.

6. **Territorial bar associations (Коллегии адвокатов)** for each region can provide complete up-to-date member lists and are included as entries where individual lawyer data was limited (e.g., #48, #60, #65, #68, #85, #95). Their emails and phones serve as reliable access points for finding additional lawyers in each region.
