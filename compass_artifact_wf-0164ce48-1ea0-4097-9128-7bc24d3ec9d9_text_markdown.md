# 30 essential legal document templates for Kazakhstan citizens

Kazakhstan's legal landscape relies on standardized document forms compliant with the Civil Code, Labor Code, and Civil Procedure Code of the Republic of Kazakhstan (ҚР). This compilation provides **30 ready-to-use legal document templates** with verified download links from official and reputable Kazakhstan sources — designed for integration into the ZanKenes AI legal consultation platform. The most reliable sources are the **Supreme Court portal (sud.gov.kz)** for court documents, **findh.org** for free DOCX downloads across all categories, **garantplus.kz** for free DOC files, and **dogovor24.kz** as the largest Kazakhstan document constructor service.

## Сенімхаттар / Powers of attorney — notarially certified delegation documents

Powers of attorney in Kazakhstan are governed by **Article 167** of the Civil Code, which limits their validity to a maximum of **3 years**. General and inheritance powers of attorney require mandatory notarial certification; vehicle powers of attorney may be in simple written form.

## Source reliability ranking and integration recommendations

The research identified a clear hierarchy of Kazakhstan legal template sources. Several originally suggested websites (**blanker.kz, docx.kz, blanki.kz, parta.kz, template.kz**) were confirmed to be **inactive or non-existent**. The verified, active sources rank as follows:

**Tier 1 — Official government sources** (highest reliability, free):
- **sud.gov.kz / office.sud.kz** — Supreme Court and Judicial Cabinet with ~20 official court document templates in DOCX/DOC
- **egov.kz** — Government e-services portal with standardized forms for civil registration and government applications
- **adilet.zan.kz** — Official legal database with regulation text and approved form specifications

**Tier 2 — Major legal template portals** (high reliability, mostly free):
- **findh.org** — Largest free Kazakhstan template catalog with direct DOCX downloads across all categories
- **garantplus.kz** — Advocate Sagidanov's free DOC library covering court documents, contracts, and employment templates
- **dogovor24.kz** — Most comprehensive Kazakhstan document constructor (subscription-based, but offers previews)

**Tier 3 — Specialized portals** (good reliability, category-specific):
- **krisha.kz** — Professional lease agreement template (PDF) from Kazakhstan's dominant real estate platform
- **potrebitel.kz** — Consumer protection complaint template (DOC)
- **defacto.kz** — Law firm with powers of attorney templates
- **kadroved.kz** — HR/employment templates including Kazakh-language versions

For ZanKenes platform integration, the recommended approach is to **prioritize direct DOCX/DOC downloads from Tier 1–2 sources** and fall back to dogovor24.kz constructor links where direct downloads are unavailable. For government application forms (templates 21–23), the platform should provide direct links to the relevant egov.kz service pages rather than standalone forms, as Kazakhstan has fully digitized these processes.

## Conclusion

This compilation covers the **30 most essential legal document templates** needed by Kazakhstan citizens for personal and civil matters. The strongest finding is that Kazakhstan's **Supreme Court system (sud.gov.kz / office.sud.kz)** provides the most authoritative court templates as direct DOCX downloads, while **findh.org** serves as the single best source for free employment and contract templates. A notable gap exists in **Kazakh-language versions** — the overwhelming majority of available templates are in Russian, with only kadroved.kz and resmihat.kz offering bilingual alternatives. For ZanKenes, auto-translating Russian templates into Kazakh using AI would significantly expand accessibility for Kazakh-speaking users who are underserved by existing template libraries.
